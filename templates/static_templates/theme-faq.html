Trouble accessing a course

If you are a Stanford student having trouble accessing ("Registering for") a Stanford on-campus course:

  1. Log out of your OpenEdx account.
  2. Navigate to the "About" page of your course
  3. Click on the big "Register" button, and you should be able to register for your course. You may be asked to authenticate with WebAuth first.
